What is Game Night?

Game night is the easiest way to plan board game nights with friends.

Get started with these 3 easy steps

Step 1: Add games to your library

The add games page allows you to add games to your library. Use the form to search for the games that you own. Click the plus on any game to add them to your digital library.

Step 2: View the games in your libary

The library page allows you to view and edit your library. Click the minus on any game to delete them from your library.

Step 3: Plan your game nights

The game nights page is where the action happens. Click the plus on the left side bar to add a new game night. After viewing the game night, click any game you own to add it to your current game night. Click the minus next to an added game to delete it from your library.
